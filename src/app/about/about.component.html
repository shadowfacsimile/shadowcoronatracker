ABOUT YACT
Why YACT?
Most of the COVID-19 trackers I was following were either filled with ads or were information heavy. I wanted a tracker that gave me just enough information about COVID-19 without ads. Hence YACT.
Sources?
For worldwide numbers Johns Hopkins CSSE github repo. And for India numbers api.covid19india.org
How up to date are the numbers?
Johns Hopkins updates their numbers once in a day around 23:59 UTC. The India numbers update is as per api.covid19india.org schedule. The numbers are NOT realtime.
